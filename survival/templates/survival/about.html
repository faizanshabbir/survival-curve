The Kaplan Meier Survival Curve provides an accurate visual representation of patient population survival over a specific period of time. In a standard survival curve, each step in the graph represents the loss of a percentage of the population at a specific time point. Survival curves have many uses which include: the comparison of a prospective treatment in patients and the comparison between patient populations of different diseases/disease types.
