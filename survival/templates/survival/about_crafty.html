Hi, We're KM Survival

A team dedicated to making you survive your curve analysis.

Born out of need.

Survival curves were previously generated using complex calculations followed by plotting points on a graph. Alternatives to manual plotting have been recently developed but are hard to use for the researcher, lack aesthetic appeal, and require the purchase of expensive software bundles.

A team of scientists and programmers at HippoGen have designed an easy-to-use tool for generating survival curves from multiple data sets. We encourage you to give it a try, and share with your fellow researchers. Happy Curving!
